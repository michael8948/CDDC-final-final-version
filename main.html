    Of course you need to spend money on the equipments, but not all of them are required. For instance, you only need to purchase the mask(50$), fins(50$), wet suit(100$) and if you want to collect the data, a diving computer(700$). Expensive stuffs can be rented from the dive shop, such as BCD, tanks, regulators and weight belt. Besides, the curriculums and fun dive experience are relatively cheap compare to your hotel fee and air tickets, so why don't give it a try?
